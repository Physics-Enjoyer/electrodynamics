Divergence#

(474)#\[\nabla \cdot \textbf{v} = \frac{\partial v_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial v_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial v_z}{\partial z}\]

Curl#

(475)#\[\nabla \times \textbf{v} = (\frac{\partial v_z}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial v_y}{\partial z})\hat{\textbf{x}} + (\frac{\partial v_x}{\partial z} - \frac{\partial v_z}{\partial x})\hat{\textbf{y}} + (\frac{\partial v_y}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial v_x}{\partial y})\hat{\textbf{z}} \]
(476)#\[\begin{split} \nabla \times \textbf{v} = \begin{vmatrix} \hat{\textbf{x}} & \hat{\textbf{y}} & \hat{\textbf{z}} \\ \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial v}{\partial z} \\ v_x & v_y & v_z \\ \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]

(477)#\[ \nabla ^2 T = \nabla \cdot (\nabla T) = \frac{\partial^2T}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial^2T}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial^2T}{\partial z} \]
